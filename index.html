How to play

  1. 🌍 Each day you get 5 places to find — spin and zoom the globe.
  2. 📍 Tap the globe to drop a pin. It's not final yet!
  3. 🎯 Drag the pin, use the nudge arrows for precision, then press Confirm — or double-tap to lock a guess instantly.
  4. 📏 The closer you are, the more points you score — up to 100 per round.
